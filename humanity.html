"Humanity"

by s.a.poetics


Human is an Intrigue intricate.
An exterior that is converse
to its interior—Infinite!
As they are both well-versed and averse.

To the Thief facing perturbation
that masses joined, to upend the chase.
To the unfaithful lover’s action
towards maidens, he shall soon disgrace.

But to the child in apology.
To the foreigner’s greet—quite himself.
And to the dreamer's success-to-be.
Complexity brought upon itself!

Yet an instance of observation
to the smallest counts or lack thereof,
one uncovers the Indication:
That a human within calls the bluff.

— June 24, 2024

Themes

The poem is set on someone vicariously inspecting the people around them. Trying to find meaning how could someone say that some people lack humanity, while even those who "lack" it can be conscious of their actions. Traversing through multiple scenarios that reveals how unique humans are from everything on earth. Where the final realization of the speaker became clear, that even for standing by, watching their individual lives, whether a prospect of a good or bad outcome---which may appear inhumane of them, there are no humans who lack humanity, only ignorance.
